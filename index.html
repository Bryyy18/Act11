Batchelor in Applied Compting Technology

The BACT is a four-year degree program that focuses on the practical aspects of the science of computing. The program develops core skills in multimedia Technology, web'mobile development and network systems administration. Futhermore, it prepares learners with supervisory and management skills. The curriculum is derived from the BS Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology with CHED Certificate of Program Compliance (COPC) No. 31, series of 2021.

Program Objectives:

The program prepare learners with the
practical skills in multimedia technology,
web mobile development and
network systems administration and
supervisorial skills to manage an organization
in this areas of competrencies, engage in a
startup business or puruse a teaching career.

Specific Objectives:

The learner should be able to:

1. Demonstrate understanding of the importance of being a ethical computing
professional when making work-related decisions.
2. Develops product or processes br applying thehir knowledge in computer or network
systems, and development of productivity tools.
3. Participate as an active member of a development service team with leadership roles
when appropriate.
4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes of products.
5. Make positive contributions to the community with the skills and abilities learned in
the program.
6. Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied
experiential and field-based opportunties.
